What are Self-Destructing Messages?

Self-destructing messages are a smart way to share information that automatically disappears after being read, just like secret agent messages in movies.

Imagine this:

  • You write a note and put it in a special envelope
  • When the recipient opens and reads it, the note immediately turns to ash
  • No traces are left behind, completely protecting privacy

This is the digital version of self-destructing messages!
