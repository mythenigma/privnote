How Privnote Protects Your Personal Privacy

1. Automatic Deletion

When you use Privnote, your messages are automatically deleted after being read. This means your sensitive personal information doesn't stay in chat history, email accounts, or on servers where it could be discovered later.

2. No Account Required

Privnote doesn't require you to create an account or provide personal information. This means your identity isn't linked to your messages, providing an additional layer of privacy protection.

3. One-Time Access

Each Privnote can only be viewed once. Once the recipient opens and reads your message, it's permanently deleted and the link becomes invalid. This ensures your personal information can't be accessed multiple times or shared further.

4. Password Protection

For highly sensitive personal information, you can add password protection to your Privnote. This ensures that even if someone gets the link, they can't read your message without the password.

5. Time-Based Expiration

You can set Privnote messages to expire after a specific time period (1 hour, 24 hours, or 7 days). This ensures your personal information is automatically deleted even if it's not read, providing additional privacy protection.
