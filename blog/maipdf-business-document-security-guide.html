Why Businesses Need MaiPDF

Modern businesses share thousands of documents daily: financial reports, strategic plans, client information, and confidential data. When these documents are shared through regular email or cloud storage, they create permanent records that can be accessed, forwarded, or compromised months or years later.

MaiPDF self-destructing PDFs help businesses protect sensitive documents by ensuring they automatically delete after being viewed a set number of times. This reduces the risk of data breaches, unauthorized access, and compliance violations.

How MaiPDF Protects Business Documents

1. Open Count Limits

Set exactly how many times your business document can be opened:

  • Board Reports – Set limit to match number of board members
  • Executive Briefings – Allow 5-10 opens for leadership team
  • Client Proposals – Limit to 1-3 views for confidential proposals
  • Financial Statements – Control access with precise open counts

2. Automatic Deletion

Once the open count limit is reached, your business document is permanently deleted:

  • No permanent record in email systems
  • Reduced risk in data breach scenarios
  • Compliance with data minimization requirements
  • Protection against unauthorized future access

3. Access Tracking

Monitor when and how often your documents are accessed:

  • Email notifications for each document open
  • Track access patterns and timing
  • Audit trail for compliance purposes
  • Identify unauthorized access attempts
