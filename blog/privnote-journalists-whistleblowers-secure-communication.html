Why Journalists and Whistleblowers Need Privnote

Journalists and whistleblowers handle some of the most sensitive information in society. Protecting sources, maintaining confidentiality, and ensuring information doesn't fall into the wrong hands is critical for both press freedom and personal safety.

Privnote self-destructing notes provide a crucial tool for secure communication between journalists and their sources. By ensuring messages automatically delete after being read, Privnote helps protect both the journalist and the source from surveillance, hacking, or legal discovery.

How Privnote Protects Journalists and Sources

1. Automatic Message Deletion

When journalists use Privnote to communicate with sources, messages are automatically deleted after being read. This means:

  • No permanent record exists in email or messaging systems
  • Information can't be discovered through legal discovery processes
  • Hackers can't access old messages if accounts are compromised
  • Sources are protected from identification through message trails

2. No Account Required

Privnote doesn't require account creation, which means:

  • No identity linking between journalist and source
  • No phone number or email verification needed
  • Complete anonymity possible
  • No tracking or logging of usage

3. One-Time Access

Each Privnote can only be viewed once, ensuring:

  • Messages can't be accessed multiple times
  • Links become invalid after first use
  • No risk of accidental forwarding or sharing
  • Maximum protection for sensitive information
